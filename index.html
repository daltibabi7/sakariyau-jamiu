Services Portfolio

Distinguished Real Estate Capabilities

Delivering regulatory compliance, risk mitigation, and optimization across all asset categories.

Property & Asset Valuation

Delivering precise, legally compliant asset valuations aligned with International Valuation Standards (IVS), IFRS, and IPSAS. We cover land, skyscrapers, machinery systems, agricultural installations, and vehicle fleets.

Property & Facility Management

Managing operations, tenant services, maintenance schedules, and rental income accounts. We integrate green technologies and energy-saving structures to maximize asset yields and ensure high occupant retention.

Asset Auctioneering

Leading transparent, competitive bidding events for liquidations, mortgage foreclosures, deceased estates, and government equipment disposal. Certified procedures guarantee maximum fair market returns.

Real Estate Agency

Fostering secure transactions in buying, selling, and leasing luxury properties. We implement extensive screening, location verification, and contract preparation to guarantee buyer and seller peace of mind.

Feasibility & Viability Studies

Providing technical, financial, and environmental appraisals to model project returns. We help developers, banking partners, and investment funds allocate capital safely in diverse market climates.

Property Rating & Taxation

Determining rental assessments, tenement rate indexes, and land taxation compliance structures. We support public agencies in generating equitable revenue and assist companies in managing rate liabilities.

Land Titling & Documentation

Navigating Governor’s Consent audits, Certificates of Occupancy (C of O) applications, Survey Plans, and title deeds registration. Direct agency coordination secures and speed-tracks official title approvals.

Strategic Consultancy

Providing advisory services on portfolio restructuring, land laws, joint ventures, and municipal zoning guidelines. We turn complex real estate scenarios into long-term strategic benefits.
