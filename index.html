+

Trading financial instruments involves a substantial risk of capital loss and is not suitable for every investor. The trading model and signal parameters presented on this website are based on rigorous historical backtesting and probability-based analysis, but contain standard limitations that all users must understand:

Market & Geopolitical Volatility

AI trading models are developed based on historical market trends. Unexpected geopolitical shifts, black swan events, or sudden monetary policy updates can render historical data ineffective and cause the model's accuracy to deviate.

Execution Speed & Latency

Even with highly accurate signal output, latency, slippage, and platform connectivity can impact your actual trade fill price, meaning real results may differ from historical simulations.

Prudent Risk Management

Past performance does not guarantee future results. There are no absolute guarantees of profitability. Never trade with capital that you cannot afford to lose, and ensure your account has proper risk controls.
